UTSA Bereans

"These were more noble than those in Thessalonica, in that they received the word with all readiness of mind, and searched the scriptures daily, whether those things were so." ~ Acts 17:11-12

The mission of the UTSA Bereans is to guide people in their relationship with God by making the truths in the bible understandable. The bible can at times, be a complex and complicated book to read, let alone understand. With UTSA Bereans, we want to equip disciples with the necessary tools needed to study, examine, and understand The Word of God.
